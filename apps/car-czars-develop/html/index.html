wELCOME TO cAR CZARS

Car Czars is a McKinney, TX based, family-owned and operated company started with the sole purpose to rent cars to Rideshare Drivers, Delivery Drivers and for Restaurants wanting to do their own food deliveries.

We started in the midst of the Covid 19 pandemic because we believe there are a lot of people who have been financially impacted, in many cases devastated, that see the gig economy as an opportunity to make extra money and start their financial recovery for themselves and their families.

We also believe there are a significant number of restaurants that have had to adapt to food delivery as a part of their revenue stream going forward as the economy starts to come back. For a number of these restaurants being able to retain more of their profits by bringing this in-house helps them to recover more quickly from the devastation of the pandemic.

By working for our neighbors and with our neighbors together we will get our economy thriving again.
